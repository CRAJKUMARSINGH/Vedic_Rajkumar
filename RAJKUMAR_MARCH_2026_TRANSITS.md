## Vedha Analysis Explanation

### What is Vedha?

Vedha (वेध) means "obstruction" or "piercing" in Sanskrit. It's a classical Vedic astrology concept where:

1. **Favorable transit + Vedha = Blocked benefits** (adverse)
2. **Unfavorable transit + Vedha = Partial neutralization** (less harmful)

### How Vedha Works

When a planet is in a favorable house, but another planet occupies its "Vedha house," the favorable results are obstructed.

**Example from March 14**:
- Moon in 7th house = Favorable for partnerships
- Ketu in 2nd house = Creates Vedha for Moon's 7th house position
- Result: Partnership benefits completely blocked

### Classical Vedha Houses (from Phaladeepika)

For Moon in 7th house, Vedha comes from 2nd house.  
This is why Ketu's position in 2nd house blocks Moon's benefits.
